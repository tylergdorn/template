This is a map of cholera deaths in the 1854 cholera outbreak in London. This map is in many ways a recreation of John Snow's original map, which displayed death locations and number of deaths. The red circles are deaths, and their size indicates the number of deaths. You can click on the individual circles to indicate how many deaths were recorded there. The blue circles are well locations. The purple circle, near the center of all the deaths, was the infected well which was the infected well responsible for the deaths.
